Our love for design and craftsmanship lead us to a chance encounter, resulting in a collaboration with New York artist Audrey Louise Reynolds to create a collection bursting with exclusive prints. Chapter One: Limited Edition

For this story, Audrey has captured single moment in time, using rain to imprint pale pink crystals blended with wild flowers, weeping willow bark with hibiscus, and mulberries with blue algae. A 50-piece limited edition collection, the hand-dyed prints feature on the finest silks cut to form fluid drapes, alongside casual designs made from the softest cotton and cashmere. No two pieces are alike, bringing exclusivity to your wardrobe.

Chapter Two: A Digital Focus

By using the same process with different ingredients, including wild blackberries, dandelion, plantain roots and pumpkin, we have remastered Audrey’s multi-coloured masterpiece by digitally printing the exclusive artwork onto metres of beautiful silk.  Silhouettes are modern, taking a relaxed approach with a feminine touch.
